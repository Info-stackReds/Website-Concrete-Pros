Everything you need to know about concrete replacement and installation in McHenry County before calling for an estimate.

Call (815) 403-7233
Standard concrete driveway replacement in Crystal Lake and McHenry County runs $6–$12 per sq ft installed. A 2-car driveway (600 sq ft) costs $4,200–$7,500. Stamped concrete driveways run $18–$28 per sq ft. All prices include demolition, base prep, pour, and finish.
Stamped concrete patios in McHenry County typically run $18–$28 per sq ft installed. A 400 sq ft stamped patio costs $8,000–$12,000 depending on pattern complexity and color options. Standard broom-finish patios start at $8–$15 per sq ft.
Most McHenry County municipalities — including Crystal Lake, Woodstock, Algonquin, and Cary — require permits for concrete driveway replacement. We handle all permit applications as part of every job. Permit fees are passed through at cost and itemized in your written estimate.
Wait at least 7 days before parking passenger vehicles on new concrete — 10 days in cooler fall temperatures. Full curing takes 28 days. For heavy trucks or loaded trailers, wait the full 28-day cure period.
Regular concrete (broom or smooth finish) is functional, durable, and the most affordable option. Stamped concrete uses patterned tools to imprint textures like slate, flagstone, or cobblestone, then colored with integral pigments or acid stains. Both are equally durable when installed correctly — stamped is purely decorative.
Most standard driveway replacements take 1–2 days for the work itself. Add 3–10 business days for permit approval. You can typically park on the new concrete after 7 days.
